What's Inside
Every Feature You Need

Database & Management

PDF Invoice Generator

Auto Balance Settlement

Excel Export & Reports

openpyxl · PatternFill · Border · Font

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Does it work without internet? +

Yes, 100%. ScreenWise Billing is a completely offline Windows application. Once installed, it never needs internet to work. Your data never leaves your computer.

Can I use it on multiple computers? +

The ₹14,000 license covers one PC. If you need it on additional computers or branches, each additional PC is priced separately at ₹6,000 per branch. Contact us for multi-branch pricing.

What happens to my data if I reinstall Windows? +

Your data is stored in a single file (billing.db) next to the .exe. Just copy that file before reinstalling and put it back after — all your patient records and invoices are restored instantly.

Can I customise it with my clinic name and logo? +

Yes, full branding customisation is included in all plans. We set up your clinic name, doctor name, address, registration number, and logo before delivering the software to you.

Is this only for physiotherapy clinics? +

No — it works for any business that bills customers. Dental clinics, general physicians, retail shops, coaching centres, service centres, wellness studios — anyone who needs professional invoices and customer billing management.

How long does setup take? +

Same day in most cases. Once you confirm the order and share your clinic details (name, logo, doctor name), we customise and deliver the EXE within a few hours. Installation takes under 5 minutes.

What if something breaks or stops working? +

All plans include free bug-fix support (2–3 months depending on plan). After that, the Annual Maintenance Contract (₹3,000/year) covers bug fixes and WhatsApp support. We also provide remote desktop help when needed.

Can I see it working before I pay? +

Absolutely. WhatsApp us and we'll schedule a free demo — either a screen share session or we can come to your clinic directly. No payment needed until you're fully satisfied.
